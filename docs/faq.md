# FAQ

### Is this really MIT? Even for competitors?
Yes. Including hotels and OTAs. We're confident the IMPT product (carbon offset + Goodness loyalty + 195 countries × 1.7M hotels) wins on its own merits.

### Why 5% and not 4% like Booking.com?
We want to win the affiliate market, not match it. 5% is the headline; we may go higher for top partners over time.

### Do I need a hotel / website / business / company?
No. Personal sites and blogs qualify. You just need a way to receive payouts (Wise, PayPal, etc.).

### How fast do bookings show up in my dashboard?
The `view` and `click` pixels fire instantly. Booking accrual lands within ~5 minutes of confirmation. Status flips on check-in and again 30 days after that (refund window).

### What if my country / city isn't in the static city list?
The widget's destination dropdown ships with 24 popular cities. Type-ahead PRs welcome. Guests can also search free-form on the lander itself.

### Do I have to display "Powered by IMPT"?
The footer shows a small "Powered by IMPT" link to swarm.impt.io. We ask you not to remove it (it's part of how we measure organic widget reach), but the licence is MIT, so you technically can. Please keep it visible — it's the polite thing to do.

### How do I get my partner key?
[partners.impt.io/widget](https://partners.impt.io/widget). Email + payout method. Approval is automated for most partners; flagged ones get a manual review.

### Can I have multiple keys?
Yes. Useful for splitting earnings across sites or tracking campaigns. Up to 10 keys per account; ask if you need more.

### What happens if IMPT goes away?
The widget code is MIT and on GitHub forever. Your keys would stop redirecting, but you'd still own the integration code. We don't plan on going anywhere — building an AI hotel system.
